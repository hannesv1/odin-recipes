Pasta

This is a very delicious pasta recipe.

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • tomato sauce
  • cheese
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 zucchini

Directions

  1. Boil the water and put the pasta in it.
  2. Cut the peper and zucchini in square pieces.
  3. Put some oil in a pot, let the vegetables bake for 1 minute and add the sauce.
  4. Add everything together and put some cheese on top.
